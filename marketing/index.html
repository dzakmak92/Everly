Good to know

Questions

Is my family's data really private?

Yes. Child, health and maternal records are stored on your device. Our servers only hold your account and billing details.

Is the pregnancy and postpartum side free?

Always. Mum&Me — including EPDS wellbeing check-ins and safety tools — is free forever.

Does it work for more than one child?

Yes, on Pro and above. Each child has their own profile, stage-adaptive tools, health records and timeline.

Which devices?

iOS, Android and the web app — your data syncs to whichever you sign in on (account only; records stay on-device).
